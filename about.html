About Drill Deep

Produced by NYU Studio 20, Drill Deep is a multimedia project that explores drill rap, a contentious hip-hop subgenre, and the role it plays in the Black community in New York City. After Mayor Eric Adamsâ€™ recent claim that the genre perpetuates violence through social media, we sought a deeper understanding of drill's connection to the communities that create it.

We elevate a variety of family, community and industry voices to shade in the sometimes superficial conversation around this music. Through these stories, Drill Deep examines the popularity of drill rap, its cultural influence, and the indelible expression of pain - often manifested as aggression and violence - prevalent throughout the art form.
